Who?

I am an associate professor of link Psychology at Brigham Young University. I am affiliated with the Clinical Psychology PhD program. I teach courses in statistics and data analysis at the undergraduate and graduate levels. I also teach clinical courses at the graduate level.

What?

My research is split into two areas. First, my primary focus is on statistical and data analysis issues in clinical psychology and the behavioral sciences. Second, my secondary focus is evaluating psychotherapy and understanding how and why psychotherapy works.
